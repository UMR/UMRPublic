Patient Records -- Benefits
—Designed to serve as the foundation of your practice’s transition to comprehensive electronic medical records.
—This permits practices of any size to take a modular approach to the implementation of clinical information technology and realize the benefits previously available only to the largest providers.    
—Knowledge Base Model (KBM) – offers your practice templates (e.g., vital signs, HPI, PFSH, surgical history) customized for numerous medical specialties
—Specificity Coding – optimizes third-party payments by indicating appropriate code levels [specifity coding] based on documentation 
—Medications Module – unique cumulative medication list which generates prescriptions via computer and checks new prescriptions preventing drug-drug, drug-allergy, drug-disease and drug-laboratory value harmful interactions
—Diagnosis Database (ICD-9) – features pre-loaded diagnosis codes 
—Procedure Database (CPT-4) – includes medical procedure codes
—HCPCS Database
—MDS Database
—Manual Lab Order Entry and Results Reporting – allows for the creation of manual lab orders and manual entry of lab results in patient charts
