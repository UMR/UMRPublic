Conclusions : Helping Health Care Catch Up
—UMR’s organizational structure can be easily used to facilitate the adoption and growth of emerging interoperative technologies. The development of regional health information organizations (RHIOs), often referred to as the building blocks of an interoperative electronic network, is essential to the vision of the Office of the National Coordinator for Health Information Technology’s (ONCHIT).
—That vision articulates the extraordinary changes necessary to transform our current system into an interconnected healthcare community.
