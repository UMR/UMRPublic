HUMAN RESOURCE COST  REDUCTION OVERVIEW
nAll US companies and corporations with 15 or more employees must comply with requirements defined in Public Law 101-336: The Americans With Disability Act.  One of these requirement is that the medical condition or history of the employee “shall be collected and maintained on separate forms and in separate medical files and be treated as a confidential medical record”.  It should be emphasized this requirement is not limited to disabled individuals or those with a special condition:  it applies to all employees and failure to comply could subject the employer to violation citations and application fines.
nFurthermore the referenced act does not define a federally-approved standard for this employees health profile, but the UMR format, Standard Guide for Providing Essential Data Needed in Advanced for Pre-hospital Emergency Services has been developed in conjunction with American Society of Testing and Materials (ASTM), and was approved on October 10th 1999.
