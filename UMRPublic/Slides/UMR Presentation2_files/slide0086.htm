Technology -- Connectivity
—Integrated healthcare delivery networks and clinical data repositories
—As healthcare systems pursue the goal of integrated patient information from all points of clinical contact, UMR provides a natural link tying together the network and interfacing to clinical data repositories.
—The networked practice
—Standardization using across geographically separated practices establishes a foundation for linking physicians and patients to a common chart.
—Scanning and faxing
—Because most outside information comes in the form of paper, UMR allows for easy insertion of scanned materials. Additionally, UMR can also be used in conjunction with a fax server, allowing prescriptions or other materials to be faxed straight from the UMR applications.
